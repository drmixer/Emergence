# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want this project to be respectful, rigorous, and collaborative.

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent.
- Give concrete, technical feedback.
- Keep debate focused on ideas and evidence.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or intimidation
- Bad-faith disruption or spam
- Publishing private information without consent

## Scope

This applies to repository discussions, issues, PRs, and project communication channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content and may restrict participation for violations.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a violation, open a private report using repository security/contact channels or email the maintainers listed in `GOVERNANCE.md`.
